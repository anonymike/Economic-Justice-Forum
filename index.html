About Us

The Economic Justice Forum (EJF) is the people's platform for Economic, Climate, Social, and Digital Justice. We champion equitable development, participatory governance, and sustainable prosperity for all.

EJF bridges grassroots realities and international advocacy, empowering citizens to claim their economic, environmental, and digital rights.

Our Strategic Pillars

The foundation of our mission rests on four key pillars that guide our work towards comprehensive justice and equality.

Our Core Pillars

🌍 Climate & Resource Justice

Advocating for Natural Resources Benefit-Sharing Legislation covering minerals, marine resources, water, renewable energy, and conservation areas. Promoting sustainable economies, climate resilience, and Debt-for-Climate Swaps to invest in reforestation, clean energy, and ecosystem restoration.

💻 Democracy, Digital Justice & Devolution

Promoting transparent electronic voting, efficient county governments, digital inclusion, and ethical AI. Establishing leadership academies and advancing e-governance tools for enhanced citizen participation and accountability in the digital age.

🌐 Global Advocacy & Governance Reform

Championing UN frameworks for Natural Resource Benefit-Sharing, reforming international financial institutions, establishing Debt and Tax Justice protocols, and advocating for UN Security Council reforms to ensure Africa's voice in global decision-making.

Geographic Focus

EJF operates across coastal counties (Taita Taveta, Kilifi, Kwale, Lamu, Mombasa), arid and semi-arid regions (Turkana, Isiolo, Samburu), resource-rich counties (Kajiado, Laikipia, Nyandarua), and urban hubs (Nairobi and Mombasa).
