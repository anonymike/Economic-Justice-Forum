About Us

The Economic Justice Forum (EJF) is the people's platform for Economic, Climate, Social, and Digital Justice. We champion equitable development, participatory governance, and sustainable prosperity for all.

EJF bridges grassroots realities and international advocacy, empowering citizens to claim their economic, environmental, and digital rights.

Our Strategic Pillars

The foundation of our mission rests on four key pillars that guide our work towards comprehensive justice and equality.

Our Core Pillars

1Ô∏è‚É£ Economic, Fiscal & Debt Justice

EJF promotes fair taxation, debt transparency, and citizen-led budgeting to ensure that public finance serves people, not power. We advocate for:

  • Debt cancellation campaigns and social audits of public borrowing and spending.
  • Progressive tax reforms that reduce inequality and fund quality social services.
  • 40% equitable allocation of the national budget to county governments.
  • 65% of county budgets dedicated to development rather than administration.
  • Participatory budgeting and citizen scorecards for accountability in all public funds.

2Ô∏è‚É£ Climate & Resource Justice

We champion equitable and sustainable management of Kenya‚Äôs natural and environmental wealth, ensuring communities benefit fairly from their resources. EJF works to:

  • Secure fair benefit-sharing frameworks for resource-rich counties and communities, covering extractives, marine, water, wind, natural gas, national parks, and ports.
  • Promote sustainable mining, renewable energy, and conservation economies that create local, climate-resilient jobs.
  • Strengthen climate resilience in arid, semi-arid, and coastal regions.
  • Integrate climate action with equity, accountability, and financial fairness through initiatives like Debt-for-Climate Swaps ‚Äî converting debt repayments into investments in:
    • Reforestation, renewable energy, and sustainable livelihoods in counties like Taita-Taveta and Marsabit.
    • Climate-smart agriculture, carbon farming, and circular economy enterprises led by youth and women.
    • Ecosystem restoration, clean water, and biodiversity protection.
  • Advocate for Direct Climate Finance flows to counties and community organizations.
  • Establish a People‚Äôs Climate Dividend, ensuring communities protecting natural resources receive guaranteed benefits.
  • Advocate globally for a UN Framework/Convention on Natural Resource Benefit-Sharing to advance peace, equity, and poverty eradication, particularly for rural grassroots and marginalized communities.

3Ô∏è‚É£ Democracy, Digital Justice, Leadership & Devolution

EJF strengthens democracy, ethical leadership, digital inclusion, and devolution as pillars of accountable governance and people-centered development. Our work includes:

  • Promoting simple, verifiable electronic voting systems for transparent elections.
  • Restructuring devolution to ensure lean, efficient, and development-focused county governments.
  • Expanding digital justice ‚Äî bridging the digital divide, ensuring fair access to digital opportunities, and protecting citizens‚Äô rights in the digital age.
  • Advocating for equitable and ethical Artificial Intelligence (AI) that serves humanity, protects the planet, and promotes equity, accountability, and sustainability.
  • Establishing a Leadership Academy for civic education, youth empowerment, and women‚Äôs leadership.
  • Advancing e-governance tools and civic tech innovations to enhance participation and transparency.

4Ô∏è‚É£ Global Advocacy & Governance Reform

EJF connects local realities to global justice movements to reshape international systems in favor of people and planet. We advocate for:

  • A UN Framework/Convention on Natural Resource Benefit-Sharing.
  • Reforms of international financial institutions (IMF, World Bank, WTO) to make global finance transparent, fair, and sustainable.
  • A UN Framework on Debt and Tax Justice to prevent exploitation and ensure responsible lending and borrowing.
  • UN Security Council reforms ‚Äî granting Africa permanent seats with veto powers and ensuring grassroots communities have a voice in global decision-making.
