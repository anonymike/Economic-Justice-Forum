EJF Kenya Climate Financing Analysis Report

Climate Finance Kenya Policy Analysis

Comprehensive analysis of climate finance flows, governance mechanisms, and community access to adaptation funds in Kenya. This report examines the current state of climate financing, identifies challenges, and provides actionable recommendations for improving access and effectiveness.

Publication Date: November 2025

Download Full Report

Executive Summary

This report analyzes the current state of climate financing in Kenya, focusing on three key areas:

  • Assessment of existing climate finance flows and distribution mechanisms
  • Analysis of governance structures and their effectiveness
  • Evaluation of community access to adaptation funds

Key Findings:

  • Significant gaps in last-mile funding distribution
  • Need for improved coordination between national and county-level initiatives
  • Opportunities for enhanced community participation in fund allocation

Research Areas

Economic Justice Research

Examining patterns of resource distribution, fiscal policies, and economic opportunities to identify barriers to equity and propose inclusive alternatives.

  • Natural resource revenue sharing
  • Tax justice and domestic resource mobilization
  • Debt sustainability and management
  • Inclusive economic growth models

Climate Justice Research

Investigating climate impacts, adaptation strategies, and climate finance governance to ensure vulnerable communities benefit from climate action.

  • Climate vulnerability assessments
  • Adaptation finance tracking
  • Community-based adaptation strategies
  • Climate policy implementation

Social & Digital Justice Research

Analyzing social inclusion, digital access, and technology governance to promote equitable participation in social, economic, and digital spheres.

  • Digital divide mapping
  • Social protection systems
  • Technology ethics and governance
  • Inclusive service delivery
